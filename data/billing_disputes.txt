Billing Disputes Policy
Document: KB-BILL-DISPUTE
Status: Published

This document outlines the policy and timelines for customer billing disputes and invoice reviews.

1. Disputing an Invoice:
Customers must notify billing support of any invoice errors or disputes within 30 days of the invoice date. Disputes filed after 30 days are not eligible for retroactive adjustments.

2. Review Procedure & Timeline:
- The Billing Team initiates an investigation within 24 hours of ticket receipt.
- A resolution, adjustment, or formal decision will be delivered to the client within 5 to 7 business days.
- During active disputes, the user's platform access will NOT be suspended, provided the undisputed portion of the bill is paid.

3. Submission:
To open a billing dispute, contact billing@platform.com with the invoice number, disputed amount, and justification.
