Account Recovery Procedures
Document Code: KB-REC-201
Version: 1.4

This document outlines the security procedures for recovering access to a user account when the primary email or credential set is lost.

1. Identity Verification Requirements:
To recover an account, the user must provide proof of identity. Acceptable proofs include:
- The last 4 digits of the payment method on file.
- The original signup date and registration location.
- An authorized domain administrator confirmation.

2. Lost Email Recovery Path:
If a user no longer has access to their registered email address, they must submit a secondary email update request. This requires validation from a billing administrator or a notarized identity statement. The support team will process these requests within 2 business days.

3. Session Revocation:
Upon successful account recovery, all active browser sessions, OAuth authorizations, and API keys are automatically revoked to prevent unauthorized concurrent access.
