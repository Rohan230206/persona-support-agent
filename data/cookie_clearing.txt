Troubleshooting Authentication Loops and Cookies
Document: KB-COOKIE-CLEAR

If you are stuck in a continuous redirect loop or login screen refresh, stale authorization cookies must be cleared.

1. Chrome Instructions:
- Click the pad lock icon in the URL address bar.
- Select 'Site Settings' or 'Cookies and site data'.
- Click 'Manage cookies and site data'.
- Click the trash icon next to platform.com to remove the session cookies.
- Refresh the browser and log in again.

2. Firefox Instructions:
- Click the shield icon in the address bar.
- Click 'Clear cookies and site data' button.
- Confirm deletion, reload page, and re-login.

Clearing cookies resolves 95% of redirect loop errors.
