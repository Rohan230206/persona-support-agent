Platform Refund Policy
Document Code: KB-BILL-REFUND

This policy explains refund eligibility criteria and standard processing timelines.

1. Eligibility:
- Subscription plans are billed in advance and are non-refundable except in cases of service outages exceeding our SLA agreement (99.9% uptime).
- Credit balances are eligible for refund upon request if the account is closed in good standing.

2. Refund Processing Time:
Once a refund is approved by the Billing Administrator, the funds are credited back to the original payment method:
- Credit Card Refunds: Take 5 to 10 business days to appear on the bank statement.
- Bank Transfer/ACH: Take 7 to 14 business days to clear.

3. Chargebacks:
If a customer initiates a chargeback or payment dispute with their bank, the associated platform account is immediately suspended pending verification.
