Payment Failures and Declined Cards
Reference: KB-BILL-DECLINE

If a payment attempt fails, the platform sends an email notice and flags the invoice.

1. Common Decline Error Codes:
- Insufficient Funds: The account has insufficient cash. Request the user to top up or use an alternate card.
- Expired Card: The card's expiration date has passed. User must update card details.
- Restrictive Lock: Bank blocked the transaction due to fraud detection. User must call their card issuer to clear the transaction.

2. Retries:
The billing system automatically retries failed invoice charges three times:
- Retry 1: 3 days after initial failure.
- Retry 2: 7 days after initial failure.
- Retry 3: 10 days after initial failure.
If all retries fail, the subscription is automatically downgraded, and platform access is restricted.
