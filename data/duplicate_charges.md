# Resolving Duplicate Billing Charges

Occasionally, processing errors can cause duplicate pending authorizations or dual charges.

## What Causes Duplicate Charges?
- Double-clicking the "Submit Payment" button during checkout.
- Browser page refresh during payment authorization.
- System retries during API network timeouts.

## How to Resolve Duplicate Charges
1. Verify if the duplicate charge is a **Pending Authorization** or a **Posted Transaction**. Pending authorizations usually clear automatically within 3 to 5 business days without debiting funds.
2. If both charges are posted (settled) transactions:
   - Contact support at billing@platform.com.
   - Provide the invoice numbers or transaction IDs.
3. The Billing team will immediately reverse the duplicate payment. The reversed transaction will appear back on your card statement within 5 to 10 business days.
